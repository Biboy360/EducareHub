WHAT IS THIS ABOUT?

EduCare is a student-focused platform that helps you manage schoolwork by sharing documents, borrowing resources, and boosting your productivity. More than just a tool, it connects learners—offering a space to collaborate, stay organized, and succeed in your academic journey together.

Wanna know more about features?

Smart progress tracker

Personalized Tool

Tier Based reward system

Points Exchange

Students can redeem their points for available products, with access depending on their current tier level. Let me know if you’d like a more formal version or a more casual version.

Pomodoro Timer & Task Manager

AcadCare helps you stay focused with a Pomodoro Timer for balanced study sessions and a Task Manager to track assignments and deadlines.

Personal Journal (Private)

The platform offers a private journal for users to reflect, vent, or track growth—fully personal and visible only to them, supporting emotional wellness without judgment.

Points Coins

Points (Coins) are earned by sharing documents, completing tasks, and using tools like the Pomodoro Timer. They can be redeemed for digital resources—and possibly physical items for local users in the future.

Mood Tracking & Community Presence

Mood Tracking lets students display statuses like “calm” or “stressed” on their profile, promoting self-awareness and quiet support within the community—without turning the platform into a distraction.

AND THERES MORE!

What is Educare Hub?
Educare Hub is a platform designed to help students track their learning progress, stay motivated, and achieve academic goals efficiently.
How do EduCare Points work?
You earn EduCare Points by completing tasks and maintaining consistency. These points unlock rewards and recognize your progress.
What is the Pomodoro timer feature?
The built-in Pomodoro timer helps you work in focused intervals, improving productivity with breaks in between.
How do leaderboards motivate me?
Leaderboards let you compete with peers, encouraging healthy competition and rewarding consistent effort and progress.
